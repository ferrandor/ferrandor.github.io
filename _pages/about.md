I am a PhD candidate in the Graduate Interdisciplinary Program in Applied Mathematics at the University of Arizona. I work with Dr. Michael (Misha) Chertkov on using tools from probability, statistics, and optimization to operate power grids at different spatial and temporal scales, and under uncertainty from both renewable and conventional generator technologies. I am broadly interested in both theoretical and practical aspects of scientific machine learning.
