My current research interests can be broadly classified as ``physics-informed artificial intelligence.'' They include:

  • Convex and combinatorial optimization

  • Distributional robustness and rare events

  • Risk-sensitive stochastic optimal control and reinforcement learning

  • Reduced order models for natural gas dynamics

  • Bayesian approaches to generative artificial intelligence

I work on a variety of networked systems that arise in engineering, primarily power transmission grids and natural gas networks. Recently, I have become interested in power distribution grids and microgrids.
